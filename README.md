# 🧊 Minimalist Workstream

**The distraction-free command center for high-stakes productivity.**

Minimalist Workstream is a lightweight, local-first Windows application designed for professionals who value deep work over digital noise. No clouds, no subscriptions, no bloat—just you and your most important task.

---

## ✨ Key Features

- **Single-Task Focus Overlay:** A subtle, non-intrusive HUD that keeps your current goal visible over all windows.
- **Deep Work Sessions:** Integrated Pomodoro-style or custom timers to track billable hours or focus blocks.
- **Local-First Privacy:** All logs and data are stored locally in `.json` format. Your workflow stays on your machine (GDPR compliant by design).
- **One-Click Stealth:** Instantly hide the interface or switch to "minimalist mode" to clear your mental space.
- **Performance Optimized:** Written to consume near-zero CPU and RAM.

## 🚀 Why Minimalist Workstream?

Modern tools like Notion or Jira are great for planning, but terrible for *doing*. They are filled with notifications and distractions. **Minimalist Workstream** acts as a layer of discipline on top of your OS, helping you reclaim your focus in a world of constant pings.

## 🛠 Installation (Beta)

1. Download the latest `.exe` from the [Releases](https://github.com/cookieoff/Minimalist-Workstream/releases) page.
2. Run the application (No installation required).
3. Set your goal and start your stream.

## ⚙️ Configuration

The app uses a simple `config.json` file for customization:
- Toggle always-on-top mode.
- Customize fonts and opacity.
- Define hotkeys for "Stealth Mode".
